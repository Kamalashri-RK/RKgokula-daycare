Our Programs

Carefully designed daycare programs that support children's learning, discipline, creativity and emotional development.

Infant Care

Our infant care program provides a calm and loving environment for babies. Caregivers focus on emotional bonding, hygiene, safe routines and early sensory development.

  • Age Group: 12 months – 24 months
  • Timings: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Meals: Parent-provided or soft baby food
  • Snacks: Milk and light snacks
  • Flexible Timing: Available based on parent schedule

Toddler Care

Toddlers are naturally curious. Our program supports their language development, motor skills and social interaction through play-based activities.

  • Age Group: 2 – 3 years
  • Timings: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Meals: Nutritious vegetarian lunch
  • Snacks: Fruits and healthy snacks
  • Flexible Timing: Half-day options available

Playgroup

Playgroup introduces children to early learning concepts through fun activities, stories, music and creative play.

  • Age Group: 2 – 3 years
  • Timings: 9:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Meals: Light nutritious lunch
  • Snacks: Fruits and snacks
  • Flexible Timing: Optional extended daycare

After School Care

A peaceful space where children complete homework, relax and participate in meaningful activities.

  • Age Group: 4 – 10 years
  • Timings: 3:30 PM – 6:30 PM
  • Meals: Evening snacks provided
  • Activities: Homework help, games, creative play
  • Flexible Timing: Available for working parents

Daycare program

A safe and caring environment where children feel comfortable, supported and engaged in meaningful activities throughout the day.

  • Age Group: 1.5 – 6 years
  • Timings: 9:00 AM – 6:30 PM
  • Meals: Nutritious vegetarian lunch
  • Snacks: Healthy evening snacks and fruits
  • Flexible Timing: Half-day and extended care options available
